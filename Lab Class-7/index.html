Paris New
Paris is the capital city of France, known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Iconic landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and Louvre Museum.
Tokyo
Tokyo is the capital of Japan, famous for its modern skyscrapers, anime culture, and traditional temples. It is one of the world's most populous metropolitan areas with vibrant city life.
New York
New York City is a major city in the USA, known for Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty. It is a cultural and financial hub with diverse communities and attractions.
